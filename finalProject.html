Final Project: "Bitmoji Tennis"

Bitmoji Tennis is based on a classic Pong game.
Outscore your opponent by hitting a ball past their racket

How to play?
  • Hit start!
  • Choose a character and level difficulty and enjoy the match!
  • The game begins with the ball in the center. Computer serves the ball randomly to start the rally.
  • The player control selected character using 'up' and 'down'buttons on their keyboard.
  • You score a point when the ball goes past opposite player's racket.
  • The ball bounces off the rackets and screen edges.
  • On 'Hard' level the ball increases the speed.
  • If you fail to hit the ball and it passes your racket, the opponent scores a point.
  • The game continues until score limit is reached
    (score limit:6 on each level)
  • The first player to reach this score wins.
  • After each point, the computer serves the ball randomly.
